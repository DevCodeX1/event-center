Frequently Asked Questions

What's your venue capacity?

Our main hall can accommodate up to 500 guests, while our smaller spaces range from 50-200 people.

Do you provide catering?

We work with several preferred catering partners and can help coordinate all your food and beverage needs.

What's included in the rental?

Basic rentals include the space, tables, chairs, basic lighting, and sound system. Additional services available.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 6-12 months in advance for large events, though shorter notice may be accommodated.
